Eleven Modules.
One Interface.

Every dimension of the market, distilled into a single dark canvas. No tabs wasted. No feature bloat. Each module exists because it earns its place.

Markets

Real-time watchlist with live quotes, volume, and change data. Global indices, equities, crypto, forex, and commodities on a single monochrome dashboard.

Chart+

Advanced candlestick charting powered by Plotly. Multiple timeframes, technical indicators (SMA, EMA, Bollinger, RSI, MACD), and institutional-grade overlays.

Supply Chain

Live supply chain intelligence sourced from SEC EDGAR XBRL filings and yFinance. Maps suppliers, customers, and sector dependencies in real time.

Flights

Global flight tracking overlay via OpenSky Network. Monitor cargo routes, executive jet movements, and aviation logistics tied to macro events.

Oil Tankers

AIS maritime vessel tracking via AISStream. Follow crude oil tanker movements, VLCC routes, and floating storage to front-run energy supply signals.

Crisis Map

Geopolitical risk heatmap. Aggregates global conflict, sanctions, natural disasters, and political instability into a single threat-assessment overlay.

Simulation

Four proprietary stochastic engines — Hermes, Kronos, Afrodita, Tartaros — running 50,000-path Monte Carlo simulations, regime detection, and stress testing.

Portfolio

Unified portfolio view with annualized returns, volatility, and Sharpe ratio. Track positions, market value, and unrealized P&L across all asset classes.

Fundamentals

Deep-dive company analysis. Valuation multiples, profitability metrics, sector classification, employee count, and full business descriptions at a glance.

Alerts

Conditional price alerts with above/below thresholds. Set custom labels, monitor multiple tickers, and never miss a critical level break again.

Risk-free simulated trading with $100K virtual capital. Execute market and limit orders, track positions, and validate strategies before committing real capital.
